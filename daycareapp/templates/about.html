ABOUT US

Located in the larger Sun City metropolis, Angels Daycare is a private, for profit organization founded in 1999. Our purpose is to provide children enrolled at the facility with an atmosphere of warmth and care.

OUR STORY

Widowed at a young age, our founder and director, Dr. Mark Antony, knows from firsthand experience the struggle single parents go thorugh to take care of their children while meeting their obligations at work. He started the daycare to cater to the needs of single fathers who just like him found themselves as the primary parent for their children for one reason or the other.

With time, the facility also became a hit with single mothers who were going through the same problem, eventually transforming into the facility of choice even for children under the care of both parents.

To date, the daycare has taken care of 10,000+ children some of whom have grown up and also used the facility for their own children.

OUR MISSION

To nurture all-rounded individuals by providing an enjoyable yet structured day for each child.

OUR VISION

To be the leading daycare services provider in the Sun City area for parents and guardians interested in a listening partner with an open line of communication concerning our children.
